The Technology Behind
Long-Term Performance

Three material innovations that solve the common door failure problems buyers face most often.

100% waterproof.
All six sides.

Moisture-resistant composite frame eliminates the rot and swelling that causes most door failures. Non-porous material tested to ASTM industry standards. The material that destroys wood and vinyl doors never gets a foothold.

  • Non-porous composite construction — moisture cannot penetrate any surface
  • Patented water-resistant draining system — no pooling at the sill
  • Lifetime warranty on frame materials — guaranteed for as long as you own your home
  • ASTM E1886/E1996 tested — certified for windstorm areas

6× better insulation
than wood doors.

CFC-free polyurethane foam core with Low-E argon glass. ENERGY STAR certified for all U.S. climate zones. Large door openings magnify energy loss — proper insulation makes a measurable annual difference.

  • R-value 5.8 vs solid wood R-value 1.0 — 6× better thermal performance
  • Tempered Low-E glass with argon fill — reduces heat transfer by ~40%
  • ENERGY STAR qualified everywhere in the United States
  • CFC-free polyurethane foam core — stable insulation over the door's lifetime

Clean it
twice a year. That's it.

Fiberglass panels won't rot or delaminate. Composite frames stay dimensionally stable across temperature extremes. Factory finish lasts for years without repainting or sealing.

  • Fiberglass won't rot, split, or delaminate — ever
  • No painting or sealing required — unlike wood doors every 2–3 years
  • Composite frames stay square — doors that fit on day one stay fitting
  • Factory finish warranted against manufacturing defects
